Mahesh Reddy

I am a Researcher at Qualcomm AI Research and working on 3D computer vision and generative AI. I completed my MSc in Computing Science from Simon Fraser University, where I was advised by Prof. Yağız Aksoy. Previously, I was a research intern at Borealis AI and received my MSc in Computer Science from the University of Manitoba, where I was advised by Prof. Yang Wang. Before that, I received my BE in Information Science from VTU, India.
